FOR SMALL BUSINESSES

Scan it. File it.
Find it later.

Sèned is a focused iOS document scanner for small businesses. Capture receipts, permits, and inventory; the App files everything into your own Google Drive under a folder you control. No backend, no analytics, no ads.

See how it works

Your Drive, your files

Sèned only ever touches files it creates. We can't see your other Drive contents — that's the drive.file scope at work.

Workspaces & categories

Group docs by company or location. Categorize by Receipts, Permits, Inventory — or your own labels per workspace.

Works offline

Capture without a connection; Sèned syncs when you're back online. Drive sync is optional.

Bilingual

English and Amharic UI, with per-workspace company names in both scripts.
